Our Story

The House of Jabri for Dates is a premium Jordanian dates company born from a passion for quality and a deep connection to the Jordan Valley - home to the world's finest Medjool and Barhi dates.

We source directly from trusted farms, grade by hand, and package in-house with the care and craftsmanship your table deserves.
