About Us

من نحن

The House of Jabri for Dates is a premium Jordanian dates company specializing in sourcing, packaging, and distributing the finest Medjool and Barhi dates from the Jordan Valley.

With direct supplier relationships and in-house packaging capabilities, we deliver exceptional quality at competitive prices. From luxury gift boxes to bulk wholesale, we serve the full spectrum of the dates market.

05 — ROADMAP

Business Phases

مراحل العمل

Start local, prove product-market fit, then expand regionally and internationally.

PHASE 1 | Months 1-6

Foundation & Local Launch

  • Register business & JFDA food license
  • Register with JODA
  • Develop brand & premium packaging
  • Source first batch: 2-3 tons Medjool + Barhi
  • Target local supermarkets in Amman
  • Social media presence (Instagram, TikTok)
  • Launch Ramadan premium gift boxes
PHASE 2 | Months 6-18

Market Penetration

  • Expand to Irbid, Zarqa, Aqaba
  • Launch e-commerce & delivery
  • Corporate gifting program (B2B)
  • HoReCa channel: hotels & restaurants
  • Invest in sorting & vacuum sealing
  • Apply for export certs (ISO, HACCP)
  • First trade show participation
PHASE 3 | Year 2-3

Regional Expansion

  • Export to Gulf (UAE, KSA, Kuwait, Qatar)
  • Gulfood Dubai participation
  • Stuffed & chocolate-coated lines
  • Scale packaging facility
  • Full team of 5-8 employees
  • Private label manufacturing
  • 50+ tons/year throughput
PHASE 4 | Year 3-5

International Scale

  • Enter EU markets (UK, France, Germany)
  • Health food channels (US, Asia)
  • Date syrup, paste, energy bars
  • Organic certification
  • 100+ tons/year, JOD 500K+ revenue
  • Airlines & duty-free partnerships

07 — SWOT

Strategic Assessment

التحليل الاستراتيجي

Strengths

  • In-house carton/packaging manufacturing
  • Direct supplier (no middleman)
  • World-class Jordan Medjool quality
  • 14% of global Medjool production
  • Low startup cost (JOD 8K minimum)
  • Year-round demand + Ramadan peak
  • Government support & growing sector

Weaknesses

  • New brand, no market recognition
  • Limited initial marketing capital
  • Seasonal production (Aug-Nov harvest)
  • Cold storage infrastructure needed
  • No export experience initially
  • Labor challenges in extreme heat

Opportunities

  • Global market growing 6.14% CAGR
  • Health trend: natural sugar alternative
  • Jordan 2030: doubling to 60K tons
  • Value-added: syrup, paste, stuffed
  • E-commerce & D2C channels
  • Free trade: EU, US, GAFTA
  • Corporate gifting market
  • Jordan imports 5K ton deficit = gap

Threats

  • Established competitors (Jordan River, Arar)
  • Water scarcity risk
  • Price competition from Saudi/Egypt bulk
  • Currency fluctuation on exports
  • Climate change impact on yields
  • Rising fertilizer & input costs
08 — REGULATORY

Licenses & Requirements

التراخيص والمتطلبات
1

Business Registration

Ministry of Industry, Trade & Supply. LLC or sole proprietorship. JOD 200-500.

2

JFDA Food License

Product registration JOD 500-1,200. Lab testing JOD 200-800. GMP required.

3

Municipality License

Operating license for facility. Health & safety inspection. Annual renewal.

4

JODA Membership

Industry access, market connections, export facilitation.

5

JSMO Standards

Codex-based food standards. Zero tolerance for violations.

6

Export Certs (Phase 2)

ISO 22000, HACCP, halal. JOD 1,500-3,000.

7

Tax Registration

Income & Sales Tax. VAT if above threshold.

8

Social Security

SSC registration when hiring. Mandatory contributions.
